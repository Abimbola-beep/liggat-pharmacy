About Ligatt Pharmacy

Committed to safe, accessible, and patient-centered pharmaceutical care.

Who We Are

Ligatt Pharmacy is a community-focused pharmacy dedicated to improving health outcomes through professional pharmaceutical services and responsible medication management.

We serve individuals and families by providing authentic medicines, health monitoring services, and expert counselling delivered by licensed pharmacists in a welcoming and confidential environment.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, safe, and patient-centered pharmaceutical care that improves the well-being of our community.

Our Vision

To be a trusted community pharmacy recognized for professionalism, quality healthcare services, and patient satisfaction.

What We Do

  • ✔ Accurate prescription dispensing
  • ✔ Blood pressure and glucose monitoring
  • ✔ BMI, height, and weight checks
  • ✔ Patient education and counselling
  • ✔ Supply of medical equipment and healthcare essentials

Professional Assurance

Ligatt Pharmacy operates in compliance with regulatory standards and professional ethics. Our services are supervised by licensed pharmacists committed to patient safety, confidentiality, and quality care.
