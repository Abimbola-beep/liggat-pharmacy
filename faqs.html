Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pharmacy services

Do I need a prescription to buy medicines? +
Yes. Some medications require a valid prescription from a licensed doctor, while over-the-counter drugs can be purchased without one.
Do you offer home delivery? +
Yes, we offer home delivery within selected locations. Orders can be arranged through WhatsApp or phone call.
Can I place an order via WhatsApp? +
Absolutely. You can send your prescription or medication request to our WhatsApp number and receive quick assistance.
What are your opening hours? +
We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Sunday hours may vary.
Can I consult a pharmacist? +
Yes. Our licensed pharmacist is available during business hours to provide professional guidance and consultations.
